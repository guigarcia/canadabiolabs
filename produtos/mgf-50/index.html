Additional Information

Description MGF stands for mechano growth factor, previously termed IGF-IEb mRNA. It’s a peptide naturally secreted in response to muscle injury and resistance training. It’s an analog of human insulin-like growth factor 1 (IGF-1), so it acts on the same receptors and has similar muscle growth stimulating effects
