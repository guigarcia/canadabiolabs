Additional Information

Description Is a synthetic brain peptide that increases cell viability by suppression of free radical levels and activating proliferative processes.. It supports brain function by reducing its peptide deficiency by supporting protein synthesis within its cells. Pinealon is recommended for anyone whose brain may deteriorate, especially the elderly and people with poor memory, attention deficit, brain injury, stroke, high stress, chronic fatigue, depression, insomnia, irritability or diseases such as Alzheimer’s, Parkinson’s, Multiple Sclerosis, Neuralgia, Neuritis, Encephalopathy and demyelinating disease. It is also useful for persons whose occupation requires full attention.
